Roast Chicken

This dish is a regional favorite, It combines the comfort of warm home cooking with the tastes from home

This dish is also well known around the world, and popular.

  • 1 or 2 pound Chicken
  • 1 tbl Thyme
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 100ml Chicken Stock
  • 1 cup Rice
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown the onion and garlic
  3. Spread the onion, garlic, and thyme on the chicken
  4. Bake the chicken for 55 minutes
