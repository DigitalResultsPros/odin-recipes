Lasagna

This dish is a regional favorite, It combines the comfort of warm home cooking with the tastes from home

This dish is also well known around the world

  • i pound Pasta
  • 1 Pound Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 2 can Tomato Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Oregano
  1. Brown the sausage
  2. Brown the onion and garlic
  3. Cook the onion, garlic, sausage, paste, and oregano
  4. Layer the meat with pasta
