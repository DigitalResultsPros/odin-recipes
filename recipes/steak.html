Steak

This dish is a regional favorite, It is the most popular meal among men

This dish is also well known around the world

  • 2 pound Ribeye steak
  • 1 sprig Thyme
  • 1 Onion browned
  • 2 tbl Butter
  1. Preheat a skillet to very hot
  2. Lay the steak slowly into the pan and leave undisturbed for 3 minutes
  3. Turn and cook for a further 3 minutes
  4. Serve the steak with the butter thyme and onion
